Color Therapy for Highcharts

 

Color Therapy was created for developers and scientists who need a seamless way to pick colors that go well together. Color Therapy currently is available for Highcharts but can easily adapt to any svg rendered chart.

It is still a work in progress.

Questions can be directed to @katychuang
